About Me

Hi, my name is Niveditha G, a Data Analytics professional with a passion for leveraging data to solve real-world problems. This experience, combined with my data analytics expertise, allows me to approach problems from both a data-driven and user-centered perspective. I hold a B.E. in Information Science & Engineering from CIT,Tumkur and have completed a comprehensive Data Analytics and Machine Learning course with ONLEI Technologies. My goal is to continue expanding my knowledge and skills in data science and analytics, contributing to impactful projects that drive business success.
